About Hanshotfirst

It all started with a dream. Just kidding. It started with a school assignment. But it has become so much more than that. Here at hanshotfirst, we hope to grow a community of Star Wars fans who love the fandom as it was made to be: for the whole family. We want to inspire this generation and the next to experience, create, and learn using their love of Star Wars as a starting point.

Whether you're here to prepare for a binge watch of The Clone Wars or to cosplay your favorite alien species, we've got you covered. Explore tons of fan-created content, including guides, recipes, recommendations, friendly-fire debates, and more!

So please, begin your journey, and may the force be with you!
